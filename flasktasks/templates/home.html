Once upon a time...

An app for storywriting.

I wanted to write a book. Not the kind of novel that lasts for generations, just a book about some of the things I've experienced in my life.

Until you try to write something, you don't realise how hard this is. So after a lifetime already spent procrastinating, and building up really a lot of skill in the area, I decided to write an app for storywriting instead

It's still very much a work in progress, but I'm becoming happy with it.

So please, if you want a way to write your story, sign up and get started! All feedback is welcome. Read the terms and conditions before you get going.
